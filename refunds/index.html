Refunds

VAI Studio early-access refunds

Request a refund within 14 days of purchase by emailing support@vychan.tech.

What to include

Send the purchase email, PayPal transaction ID, product or SKU, VAI Studio version, purchase date, and a short description of the issue. Never send a password or payment credential.

How requests are handled

When appropriate, support may first offer installation or activation help. Eligible refunds are returned through the payment provider. A refunded or charged-back purchase may have its paid entitlement and signed receipt revoked.
