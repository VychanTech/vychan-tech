Privacy at VychanTech

This notice describes the information used to operate VychanTech accounts, VAI Studio access, purchases, and support. Last updated July 11, 2026.

Information we handle

Account email and optional display name; salted password-verification data; email-verification, session, and security records; checkout, purchase, campaign-attribution, entitlement, and receipt records; device-link label and app version; support messages; and operational audit records. Campaign measurement may record anonymous aggregate page-view and call-to-action event counts with a campaign identifier, event type, allowlisted page path, and time; those event rows do not contain an account ID, email, cookie, IP address, user agent, or fingerprint. Session security and rate limiting may separately use hashed IP address and user-agent values.

Why we use it

To create and secure accounts, process purchases, deliver and restore access, link VAI Studio, prevent abuse, answer support requests, process refunds or disputes, and maintain financial and security records.

Service providers

Cloudflare provides site, account, database, and security infrastructure. PayPal processes payments. Transactional email providers may deliver verification and account messages. Their own terms and privacy notices apply to information they process.

Choices and contact

Do not send passwords, OAuth tokens, payment secrets, or private app databases to support. To request access, correction, or deletion, email support@vychan.tech. Some purchase, fraud-prevention, dispute, and legal records may need to be retained.
